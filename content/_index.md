I am a software engineer focused on distributed systems and the infrastructure required to run products at scale. Currently, I am a Senior Software Engineer at Coinbase, where I architect resilient systems for our onboarding platform, evolving legacy infrastructure into event-driven services in Go to handle global compliance and high-throughput traffic.

My background is rooted in the intersection of system reliability and product engineering. During my five years at Google, I managed large-scale initiatives, including migrating hundreds of millions of users across storage platforms, designing internal A/B testing frameworks, and strengthening infrastructure to support global privacy standards. I began my career at Samsung working on low-level camera software, which shaped my perspective on how hardware constraints influence high-level system performance.

An IIT Dhanbad graduate, I am particularly interested in the operational side of AI infrastructure, specifically RAG pipelines and the Model Context Protocol, and building tools that improve developer productivity.
